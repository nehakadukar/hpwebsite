Cancellations and Refunds

Change or Cancel An Order

Can I cancel or change my order?

Orders can only be cancelled before shipping. Please call our HP Shopping Call Centre on
1800 180 1810 to cancel an order. A cancellation fee of 5% of the order value will be applicable.

Refunds

All refunds against order cancellations after adjusting the cancellation charges, will be
routed through the original payment process such as payment gateway CC Avenue or banks in
case of EMI offers. However if there is any change in order and the differential amount if
any to be refunded may be sent through a cheque payment at the discretion of HP

Returns Policy

How do I return my order?

If the shipment was also initiated at the time of cancellation request, an additional
charge of 2% of the order value or Rs.200/- (Rupees Two Hundred only) whichever is higher
will be charged in addition to the cancellation charges of 5% of the order value.
