Coverage Extension

Go beyond your standard warranty with a Care Pack service that works best for you.

In-Warranty

In-Warranty extensions include a variety of Care Pack services to enhance your printer’s standard warranty for better service and faster resolutions. Available in 2 to 5 year service terms.

All In-Warranty services include remote problem diagnosis and support, plus free shipping and returns.

You can purchase In-Warranty services for your printer at time of purchase or up to 90 days after printer purchase.

Post-Warranty

Post Warranty Care Pack services take over after your standard warranty or your upfront Care Pack service expires. Available in 1 or 2 year service terms.

Includes remote problem diagnosis and Next Business Day Exchange service.

You can purchase Post Warranty services for your printer 90 days before or 30 days after your standard warranty or Care Pack service expires.
